## Key Learning

An LLM is only the reasoning engine of an AI application. A production AI system requires architecture around the model, including data ingestion, retrieval, permissions, memory, APIs, monitoring, deployment, and security.

The most important lesson from Day 1 was:

> Understand the problem before choosing the framework.
